Delete Your Account

HealthPilot - Navigate Your Health Journey Worldwide

This page explains how to delete your HealthPilot account and the personal data associated with it.

1. Delete your account from within the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the HealthPilot app:

  1. Open the HealthPilot app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action (you may be asked to re-enter your password to verify it's you).

Your account and data are deleted immediately and permanently once you confirm.

2. Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, you can request deletion by email. Send a message to mahesh.selfdev@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account with the subject "HealthPilot Account Deletion Request".

We will verify your identity and delete your account within 30 days of the request and confirm by email once it's done.

Or submit a request here

Enter the email address linked to your HealthPilot account and we'll process the deletion within 30 days.

Changed your mind? Requests are processed within 30 days, so if you cancel before then your account stays active. To cancel a pending request, email mahesh.selfdev@gmail.com from the same address with the subject "Cancel Account Deletion Request".

3. What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile information (name, email, age, gender, blood type, height, weight, conditions)
  • Medicines, schedules and adherence records
  • Health logs — blood sugar, blood pressure and weight readings
  • Sleep, step, water and food/nutrition logs
  • Mood and mental wellness entries
  • Goals and achievements
  • Appointments and emergency contacts
  • Family group membership and shared health data
  • Notifications and registered device/notification tokens
  • Subscription records

4. What may be retained

We may retain a limited amount of data where required by law, for fraud prevention, or for tax/financial records relating to any purchases (e.g. subscription transaction records). Such data is kept only as long as legally required and is not used for any other purpose. Purchase receipts managed by Google Play are governed by Google's own policies.

This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your health data cannot be recovered. If you want to keep a copy, export or note down your records before deleting your account.

5. Contact

For any questions about account or data deletion, contact us at mahesh.selfdev@gmail.com.

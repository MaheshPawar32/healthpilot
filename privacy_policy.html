Privacy Policy

HealthPilot - Navigate Your Health Journey Worldwide

Last Updated: June 2025
Effective Date: June 2025

1. Introduction

HealthPilot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our HealthPilot mobile application ("App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Age, gender, blood type, height, weight
  • Health Conditions: Medical conditions you choose to enter

2.2 Health Data

  • Blood sugar readings (mg/dL, mmol/L)
  • Blood pressure readings (systolic/diastolic mmHg)
  • Weight measurements (kg/lbs)
  • Medicine schedules and adherence records
  • Sleep patterns and quality data
  • Water intake and nutrition logs
  • Mood entries and mental wellness data
  • Step count and fitness activity data

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage analytics (Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads via AdMob)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Track and display your health metrics and trends
  • Generate health risk assessments (Crisis Predictor)
  • Send medicine reminders and health notifications
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (free tier only)
  • Analyze app performance and fix issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your health data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage (Hive database). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • Local device encryption for stored data
  • Optional biometric app lock (fingerprint/face)
  • Private mode to hide sensitive information

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement display (free tier only)
  • Google Fonts: Typography rendering

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

6. Advertising

Free tier users will see advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized based on your interests. Pro subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

HealthPilot is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Export: Export your health data in standard formats
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

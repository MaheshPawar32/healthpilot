What is HealthPilot?

HealthPilot is a personal health companion to track medicines, blood sugar and blood pressure, weight, steps, sleep, mood and goals — with reminders and an optional family health overview.

Is my health data private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely against your account and is only visible to you — unless you explicitly accept a family invite, which shares a read-only health overview with that person. You can stop sharing at any time. See our Privacy Policy.

How do medicine reminders work?

Add a medicine with a dose time and frequency (daily, once, every other day, or weekly). HealthPilot reminds you at that time — both on your device and as a push notification — and only once you've taken it for the day. Reminders fire on every device you're signed in on.

Why aren't my steps counting?

  • Grant the Physical activity permission when prompted (or from the Fitness screen's banner).
  • Steps are read from your phone's built-in step sensor; if your device has none, you can enter steps manually.
  • Steps taken while the app is closed are counted the next time you open it.

How does family sharing work?

Invite a family member by email and choose their relation. They must accept the request before any data is shared. Once accepted, you each see a live read-only health overview of the other. Free accounts can link 1 member; Pro allows up to 5.

What does Pro include?

Pro unlocks unlimited medicines, up to 5 linked family members, and other premium features. Subscriptions are billed through Google Play and renew automatically; you can cancel anytime from your Google Play account.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → HealthPilot → Cancel. You keep Pro until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all your health data. This cannot be undone.
